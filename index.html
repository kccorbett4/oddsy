What MyOddsy does

  • Positive expected value (+EV) picks — every market is compared to a Pinnacle-anchored vig-free median so you see only bets with a real mathematical edge.
  • Odds comparison across 21 US books — DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, BetRivers, Fanatics, ESPN BET, Hard Rock, Bally Bet, BetParX, William Hill US, Fliff, Rebet, Bovada, BetOnline, MyBookie, BetUS, LowVig, BetAnySports, GTBets, Everygame.
  • Sharp play detector — composite scoring combines market discrepancy, EV strength, consensus divergence and underdog value into a 0-100 confidence score.
  • Arbitrage finder — scans every bettable book pair for guaranteed-profit price mismatches across moneylines, totals, and spreads.
  • Home-run & player props — Statcast-backed MLB HR prop scanner with book-by-book best price.
  • Correlated parlays — flags same-game parlay legs that logically pair (favorite + over, underdog + under) with EV calculated per leg.
  • Reverse line movement & stale lines — detect books lagging behind sharp consensus so you can grab the old number before it moves.
  • Strategy builder — define your own scoring rules and backtest against the live slate.
